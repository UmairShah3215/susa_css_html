Flexport.

"Flexport is bringing modern technology to freight forwarding and customs brokerage. Entrepreneurs like Ryan don't come along very often, so when you see one, you get on board."

Stedi.

"Stedi has created an amazing b2b structured messaging platform. Millions of businesses send each other invoices, payment notifications, updates, and other messages using a 1960s (!) protocol called EDI. Stedi takes all of the pain out of working with EDI, making it much easier for businesses to transact and communicate with each other programmatically."

Leo Polovets, Partner, Susa Ventures

Seth Berman, Partner, Susa Ventures

Chad Byers, Partner, Susa Ventures

c.   ————   philosophy

Our belief.

We believe that entrepreneurs are the lifeblood of progress, and we exist to help
them realize their visions for a better world.

our approach

n all we do, we leverage the Susa Family, a deeply embedded community of
founders, investors, partners & advisors. We focus on building better ways for
everyone in the family to connect, share and learn from each other.

what we invest in

We invest in businesses with strong compounding moats, such as proprietary data,
economies of scale and network effects.

We are sector agnostic and have made multiple investments in areas such as
Enterprise Software, Fintech, Logistics, Healthcare, Consumer & Frontier Tech.

d.   ————   experience

The Susa experience.

We strive to be the most accessible and supportive early-stage investor. Joining the Susa Family means joining a deeply connected community of founders, investors, partners & advisors, and access to the following:

community

"Susa Ventures has been an extension of my founding team. They introduced some of Flexport's most important early customers, followed on in every financing round. Susa has been hands-down one of Flexport's most engaged, connected, and helpful investors." Ryan Petersen, Founder and CEO of Flexport

We partner with domain experts to host Susa Bootcamps, one hour webinars on critical early-stage startup topics, including storytelling, recruiting and leadership. Our Family Dinner series brings together intimate peer groups with a common thread (stage, sector, role) to deepen connections and share best practices, resources and insights.

Mountain Tech Summit is a one-of-a-kind gathering that brings together 100+ Susa CEOs and top tier investors for 3 days of mountain activities and inspiring content.

resources

Leveraging the scale of our portfolio, we secure Susa Deals, service provider discounts, providing $100K+ in avg. savings.

The Susa Job Board aggregates job postings across the portfolio so that we can amplify open roles across our networks.

Susa Family News is our quarterly newsletter, announcing the latest portfolio and firm news, and sharing new resources.

We help with Series A fundraising, yielding the highest top-tier follow-on rate among micro-VCs, over 2x industry average.

"From thinking through our storytelling and refining our deck, to introducing us to Series A funds, their team truly rolled up their sleeves and helped spearhead the process. We feel lucky to partner with Susa on this journey and look forward to years of working together." Sean Henry, Co-founder and CEO of Stord

Susa Ventures is named after a Mountain Gorilla family in Rwanda called the Susa Family. These gorillas are extraordinary (and awesome!), as they were one of the first species on earth to organize into strong family groups. This idea of bringing together a unique group of individuals to form a strong family and community was the genesis behind the fund. Come join the family!

e.   ————   fellowship

venture fellows program.

a one-of-a-kind, five month program for aspiring
venture capital investors.

Applications are now closed.

mission

Venture capital is an important part of the economy. It provides funding to big, risky ideas. Those ideas, and the teams behind them, have changed the world. Apple, Google, Amazon.

Ideally, VC should be a meritocracy, funding the best ideas and teams. In practice, this isn’t always the case. Networks play a huge role in who and what gets funded. While there are many ways to help improve this reality, Susa believes it starts with who is making the investment decisions.

Driven by this belief, we’re thrilled to announce the Venture Fellows Program, a five month training program for aspiring venture investors.

details

How long will the program be?

Five months, September 20, 2022 — March 7, 2023

When is the application deadline?

11:59pm PT on August 21, 2022

Where can I find the application?

Applications are now closed.

Who should apply?

Any aspiring venture capital investor, within the US or Canada, regardless of educational background, gender identity, race, sexual orientation, upbringing, or professional experience.

How many Venture Fellows will there be?

8-10

What is the time commitment for this program?

This is a part-time, but very involved, program. The more Fellows put into it, the more they will get out of it. Fellows will meet as a group once every other week for one hour, in addition to one-off diligence meetings with the Susa investors based on the pace of deals, likely 5 hours per week during PST working hours. This program will be entirely virtual.

Will Venture Fellows be compensated?

Venture Fellows will receive a monthly stipend to compensate for hours they may need to take off from work.

If you’d like to learn more, you can read about the origins of this program in the medium posts below.
